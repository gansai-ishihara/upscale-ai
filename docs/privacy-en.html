Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

UpScale AI ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled within the App.

1. On-Device Processing

All video super-resolution processing is performed entirely on your device. No video data, image data, or processing results are ever sent to any server.

2. Data We Collect

The App may collect the following data:

  • Advertising Data: Through the Google AdMob SDK, IP address (used for approximate location estimation), crash logs, performance data, and advertising identifiers (with your permission) may be collected for ad delivery and optimization.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchase status is managed through the App Store (StoreKit). Payment processing is handled by Apple; the App never accesses your credit card or payment details.
  • Usage Data: Daily processing counts are stored in local storage on your device. This data is never transmitted externally.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For ad delivery. Google Privacy Policy

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties.

4. Tracking

The App supports Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework on iOS 14.5 and later. You can choose whether to allow tracking. All features remain fully functional regardless of your choice.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored processing history can be deleted from within the app settings. Uninstalling the App removes all data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page.
